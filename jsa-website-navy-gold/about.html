Who we are

Building trust through integrity, knowledge and experience

We, Jolly Stephen & Associates (“JSA”), are a Chartered Accountant firm built on values and beliefs powered by integrity, knowledge, expertise and experience — based in Kerala, India, with clients in India and abroad.

We offer a multitude of professional services including Audit & Assurance, Taxation, Corporate Services, Virtual CFO, Financial Advisory, Risk Management, Process Management & Accounting, ERP Implementation & Support and Systems Risk Management & IT Security Services.

Our firm represents a coalition of specialised skills geared to offer sound financial solutions and advice. Our team comprises professionals with specialised skills coupled with service techniques, equipped to meet the changing environment of business across different sectors. We are committed to add value and optimise the benefits accruing to clients.

Our approach

A thorough, risk-based approach to audit

We audit financial statements prepared in accordance with Indian Accounting Standards (“Ind-AS”) based on Auditing and Assurance Standards (“AAS”). We make use of technology to ensure the process of information gathering and dissemination is as open and efficient as possible, and to help drive audit efficiencies for your ultimate benefit.

We pride ourselves on our thorough and risk-based approach. We ensure our audit effort is focused on high-risk or subjective accounting issues, allowing us to resolve significant issues early — resulting in less management distraction. We achieve this by starting planning procedures early and investing appropriate time in understanding your business, through meetings across every level of the organisation.

Our purpose is to obtain as much comfort as possible from your internal control framework so that we can take the most efficient audit approach. Quality is at the core of everything we do at JSA — and we believe it is not just about reaching the right opinion, but how we get to that opinion.

Our heritage

In the age of discovery, trade ships sailed from the shores of Europe carrying goods to be bartered in the Far East and the New World. The merchants found the voyages exciting, but were keen not to be cheated … thus, our profession was born. “Auditors” (from the Latin verb audre, to listen) spent their time aboard these ships, in the thick of each transaction, gaining a profound understanding of the trade.

Nothing has changed. Our audit is still based today on a profound understanding of our clients’ business, their risks and their future outlook. We listen to our clients. Our ability to distil the essence of what they do brings rigour to our audit and a unique perspective. Simple, really.
