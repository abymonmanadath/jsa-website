Founder & Chief Executive Officer

CA Jolly Stephen

B.Sc, FCA, CPA
Fellow Member, ICAI (FCA) Associate Member, CPA Ireland 25+ Years Experience
+91 80782 85658 +91 484 2997250 cajollystephen@gmail.com

CA Jolly Stephen has 25 years of experience in Business Advisory, Audit, Taxation, Accounts and Finance. He is a Fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (FCA) and an Associate member of the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Ireland (CPA).

His vast international experience — most notably in the UAE, Bahrain and Qatar — together with his flexibility and adjustability to different business environments and cultures, has given him an international perspective over the years. He has led numerous high-profile clients and developed expertise across construction, manufacturing, investment, banking, NBFC, insurance, real estate, hospitality and retail sectors.

His international exposure in Business Reengineering and his expertise in financial management and business advisory ensure our clients receive top-quality service coupled with personal attention to their specific needs.

Head of Business Advisory

Mr. Manoj Thomas

Engineering Graduate, MBA, PhD (Pursuing)
Business Transformation Start-Up Ecosystem Global Experience

Mr. Manoj Thomas is the Head of Business Advisory, known for his visionary leadership and strategic expertise. He holds an Engineering degree and a postgraduate qualification in Business Administration, providing a strong foundation for his multifaceted career. Currently pursuing a PhD with a focus on the Start-Up Ecosystem, he is deeply invested in innovation and entrepreneurial growth.

With decades of experience, Mr. Thomas has served in top management positions at reputed multinational corporations in India and overseas. His global perspective and operational acumen have been instrumental in driving business transformation across industries. A sought-after mentor, he actively guides numerous business organisations worldwide, contributing to their sustainable success.

His insights on leadership, technology integration and business scalability have earned him high regard in the professional community. Passionate about empowering the next generation of entrepreneurs, he remains a dynamic force in the evolving global business landscape.
